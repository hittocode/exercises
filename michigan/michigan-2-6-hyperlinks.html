Relative Reference

Relative References (sometimes called internal links) connect to other pages in other domains. In the href attribute of the tag only the folder and filename is specified. Some examples of when you might use relative references would be in your navigation bar or links ot your images.

-------targets------- -anchors can take a target attribute - _self -default action - _blank - open in a new tab or window - _top and _parent --------review---------- - a page without links is extremely rare - links can be absolute, relative and internal (#) - use caution when using images (accessibility)
